Sound Sultan

Life and death of the legend

Here is the life of Sound Sultan

  • 1976 - Sound Sultan (Olanrewaju Abdul-Ganiu Fasasi) was born November 27, 1976
  • 1991 - He had taken an interest in music, miming at school parties and writing his own lyrics. He attributed his early influence to his elder brother Baba Dee who studied theatre arts, and received most of his early stage experience through Baba Dee.
  • 1999 - He started learning how to play the guitar after his secondary school education and was in a band.
  • 2000 - He released his first single Mathematics "Jagbajantis".
  • 2009 - Sound Sultan married his long-term girlfriend Chichi Morah, now known as Farida Fasasi. They had three children
  • 2012 - It was announced that Sound Sultan was made a UN Ambassador for Peace for his exemplary lifestyle and career
  • 2015 - Sound Sultan released the rap single "Remember" after a long absence from the music scene.
  • 2021 - It was reported that Sound Sultan was somewhere in the USA receiving chemotherapy as he was reportedly diagnosed Angioimmunoblastic T-cell lymphoma in the throat. He died July 11, 2021 at the age of 44 as a result of complications from the illness.

Sound Sultan was regarded as one of the pacesetters of modern hip hop music in Nigeria.
He was notable for using his music to speak against poverty, corruption, bad governance and societal ills in Nigeria.
